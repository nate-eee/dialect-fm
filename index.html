Dialect is a multidisciplinary research project. We aim to create a theoretical framework for understanding the ideal conditions where collaborative human relationships lead to emergent outcomes, such as creativity, innovation, and novel "0 to 1" breakthroughs. In a world where digital intelligence is rapidly catching up with human intelligence, Dialect represents our conviction in humanity’s unique capability and role in the coming years.
